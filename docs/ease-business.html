Data source & citation

Ease of Doing Business data is drawn from the World Bank Doing Business database, accessed via the World Bank Open Data API. The Doing Business project measured regulatory environments for small and medium enterprises across 190 economies. The programme was discontinued on 16 September 2021; the latest available observations are from the 2019 edition (published 2019).

Country group memberships (UN M49 regions, World Bank income groups, UN SDG groups, OECD DAC groups, WB FCS status) are sourced from the Country Classification Commons library and joined to the Doing Business data by ISO3 code.
