Why this matters

The same SDG score can tell different policy stories depending on whether you slice countries by UN regions, World Bank income classes, OECD DAC groups, FCS status, or SDG regional clusters. This lab demonstrates that classification choices are not cosmetic: they change conclusions, rankings, and peer comparison sets.
